About

  • ScanHealth is a web application that offers free diagnosis of pneumonia. The client only needs to upload an x-ray photo of the chest region and wait a few moments for the diagnosis. Examples of x-ray images are shown below and illustrate both normal and pneumonia affected lungs.


  • ScanHealth has an advanced Artificial Intelligence technology that can accurately detect pneumonia patterns in x-ray exams. The algorithm takes only a few seconds to run and classify the patient's clinical condition, safely and privately. The dataset used in the project was extracted from this website and consists of historical x-ray images of patients diagnosed with or without pneumonia. Among patients who were diagnosed with pneumonia, the predictive model was able to correctly diagnose more than 90% of pneumonia cases (recall).
